Our Story

Where Every Morning Begins with Love

Yash Poha Point was born from a simple dream — to bring the authentic taste of Madhya Pradesh's beloved breakfast right here to Gonglai. Starting with just a small stove and a big passion, we've been serving hot, fresh poha to our community every morning without fail.

Every plate is prepared from scratch using hand-selected ingredients. From the fluffiness of the rice flakes to the crunch of roasted peanuts and the warmth of our secret spice blend — every bite tells our story of dedication and love.
